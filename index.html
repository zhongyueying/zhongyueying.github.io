Précédent Suivant

Yueying Zhong 1960 Born in Heilongjiang Province, China He received his B.A and MFA from Luxun Academy of Fine Arts in China. He was an associate professor of Luxun Academy of Fine Arts before he came to USA in 1995. He also worked as an editor of Fine Arts, one of the most prestigious art journals in China. One of his thesises won the Wu Zuoren International Fine Art Exchange Foundation Award in 1991. His painting received the Special Oversea Grand Prize at the Asian International Ink and Brush Painting Exhibition in Tokyo, Japan in 1993. He has solo exhibitions in Fuontbound University, William Wood University, Stephen College, University of Missouri, Saint Louis University Museum of Art, and Missouri State Museum in USA. In China, he has held his exhibitions in Art Museum of Luxun Academy of Fine Arts, Tsinghua University Art Museum, Shenyang University Museum of Arts and Zhongbai Art Museum. He has published more then 70 articles in various art periodicals in China and Taiwan. He is the author of four books: The Comparative Chronicles of Major Events in Chinese and Western Art History, Charm & Style, Breakthrough in Art Creativity and The Original Art Creation. He is a guest professor of Luxun Academy of Fine Arts, Currently lives in Marin County located in the San Francisco Bay Area in California, USA.
